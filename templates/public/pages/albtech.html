Our Story

The Problem We Saw: In 2019, we witnessed a world drowning in disconnected contacts, unreliable networking, and broken trust in professional relationships. Contact lists were cluttered, networking was inefficient, and finding verified professionals felt impossible.

The Solution We Built: Litarch emerged as the answer—combining cutting-edge VCF technology with AI-driven intelligence and category-based communities. We didn't just create another contact manager; we built the foundation for trusted global networking.

Our Growth: From a small team with a big vision, we've grown to serve 250,000+ verified professionals across 85+ countries, processing millions of trusted contact exchanges while maintaining 99.9% uptime and pioneering new standards in contact technology.
